Community

In addition to the dozens of applications and games that are available on this site, an number of other Qt Palmtop environment (a.k.a. QPE) software is available. Links to this are available on the Links page.

The forum for topics specific to Qt Palmtop is the QPe-devel mailing list. Subscribe here.

The forum for topics specific to Qt/Embedded or to Qt Palmtop is the Qt-Embedded-Interest mailing list. Subcribe to that list by sending the word subscribe to qt-embedded-interest-request@trolltech.com. Unsunbscribe by sending unsubscribe.

The forum for general Qt discussion is the Qt-Interest mailing list.
